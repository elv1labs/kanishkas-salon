## 🚀 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the website and click **"Login"** in the top navigation bar.
2. Click **"Create an account"** at the bottom of the login form.
3. Fill in your:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Mobile number *(optional, used for SMS reminders)*
   - Password *(minimum 8 characters)*
4. Click **"Register"**.
5. Your account is now active with **CLIENT** access.

> **Tip:** You can also sign in with your **Google account** in one click using the "Continue with Google" button.

## 📅 Booking an Appointment

### Step-by-Step Booking

1. Click **"Book Now"** on the home page, or navigate to **Services → Book**.
2. **Choose a Service:** Browse categories or search for your desired treatment.
3. **Select a Date:** Pick an available date from the calendar.
4. **Select a Time Slot:** Available slots for that date are shown.
5. **Choose Staff:** Select a preferred staff member (optional — we'll assign the best available if not selected).
6. **Add Notes:** Any preferences, allergies, or special requests.
7. **Review & Confirm:** Check your booking details and total amount.
8. **Payment:** Pay the deposit online via card (Stripe) or choose to pay in full at the salon.
9. **Confirmation:** You'll receive a confirmation via email and SMS with your unique **Booking Reference**.

### Managing Your Appointments

Navigate to **Dashboard → Appointments** after logging in.

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| View upcoming appointments | Login → Dashboard → Appointments |
| Cancel an appointment | Click "Cancel" next to the appointment (subject to cancellation policy) |
| Re-book a service | Click "Book Again" from your booking history |

> **Cancellation Policy:** Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation charge. Refer to the salon's full cancellation policy on the website.

## 🛍️ Shopping for Products

### Browsing Products

1. Click **"Products"** in the top navigation.
2. Filter by **category** (Hair Care, Skin Care, Makeup, etc.).
3. Click any product card to view full details, ingredients, and usage instructions.

### Adding to Cart & Checkout

1. On any product page, click **"Add to Cart"**.
2. Continue shopping or click the **🛒 Cart icon** in the navigation.
3. In the cart, you can:
   - Adjust quantities
   - Remove items
   - Apply a **Gift Voucher code**
   - Redeem **Loyalty Points** for a discount
4. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**.
5. Enter your **delivery address**.
6. Complete payment via **Stripe** (card/UPI).
7. You'll receive an order confirmation email.

### Tracking Your Order

- Navigate to **Dashboard → Orders** to see all your orders and their statuses.
- Order statuses: `Pending → Processing → Shipped → Delivered`

## 🎁 Gift Vouchers

### Purchasing a Gift Voucher

1. Go to **Products → Gift Vouchers**.
2. Select your desired value.
3. Add to cart and complete purchase.
4. You'll receive the voucher code via email.

### Redeeming a Gift Voucher

1. During checkout (appointment or product order), enter the **voucher code** in the designated field.
2. The voucher value is applied to your order total.
3. Partial redemptions are supported — the remaining balance stays on the voucher.

> **Note:** Gift vouchers have an expiry date. Check the expiry on your voucher email.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I book an appointment without creating an account?**  
A: Currently, an account is required for online booking so we can send you confirmations and manage your booking history.

**Q: How long does delivery take for products?**  
A: Standard delivery within Indore is 2–3 business days. For other locations in India, allow 5–7 business days.

**Q: Can I change or cancel my appointment?**  
A: Yes, from Dashboard → Appointments, up to 24 hours before your appointment time.

**Q: Do my loyalty points expire?**  
A: Points may expire after a period of inactivity. Check the loyalty rules displayed in Dashboard → Loyalty.

**Q: Can more than one person use a gift voucher?**  
A: Gift vouchers are single-use codes but support partial redemptions — the remaining balance is preserved for the next visit.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. We use industry-standard security (bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS, Stripe for payments). We do not sell or share your personal data.

**Q: How do I cancel my account?**  
A: Contact us at kanishkasen100@gmail.com and we will process your account deletion within 7 business days.
